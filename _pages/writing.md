## Writing and Mental Health

I often write for my mental health, as I find it helps me process things.

- I discuss this in a few blog posts, including [How Writing Saved My Life](https://mdsimpson.co.uk/2022/01/how-writing-saved-my-life/).

I also write stories based on my experiences with mental health.

- Check out [The Journey](https://mdsimpson.co.uk/2020/05/the-journey/), a short story shortlisted in a competition by Mind, the mental health charity.
- I also discuss the inspirations behind that story in [this blog post](https://mdsimpson.co.uk/2019/06/writing-the-journey/).
