As someone who has clocked over 800 hours in the first Cities Skylines, I was naturally quite excited for the release of Cities Skylines 2. And, like most people, I was left thoroughly underwhelmed by the game we were given.

However, rather than writing about it, I thought I'd have a go at making some video essays about the game on YouTube. It started with a First Impressions video back in November, and I've been trying to keep up-to-date with the situation over the last six months.

The latest video is online now. In it, I try to test whether starting a new city on a new map and with a new theme makes any difference to the game. You can view it below or watch the full playlist on YouTube here.
