For this weeks Photography Feature, I thought I'd share some of my favourite pictures that I've taken in my home city: London, England.

### London

I was very young when we moved away, but we've been back at least once every year since to visit friends and relatives. The city has always felt more like home than anywhere I have ever been, so there is definitely a special connection. It is also, fortunately, full of wonderful buildings, bridges, monuments and other shiny things for a photographer to take pictures of!
