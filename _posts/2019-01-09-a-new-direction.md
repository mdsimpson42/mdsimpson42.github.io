I haven't posted on here for a while now. I used to write a lot about gaming, as that was my main hobby and chosen profession, but now I have a new job (and have left the games industry), which means I can't keep up with the latest titles and write full reviews (not that I was ever much good at doing that anyway!).

I considered abandoning the blog, but I have instead decided to take things in a new direction. In this post, I discuss the plans that I have for the blog over the upcoming months.

There will still be video game-related posts. In fact, now that games are my hobby rather than my job again, I am hoping to write more about the games that I actually play for fun. However, I have decided to post about a few other things that matter to me as well.

### Research & Technology

Some of what I want to write about relates to my new job as a university researcher. This will include lots of stuff about technology and green energy, which - as well as being tangentially related to my work - includes a lot of positive news (something which I think most of us need at the moment!). I have recently changed my news/social media diet and this has helped to improve my general mood and my outlook on the future, so I thought I'd share some of this stuff here.

### Mental Health

Continuing on that theme, I suffered from a range of mental health issues over a prolonged period of time, which is part of the reason why I haven't kept this blog up to date. Now that I am mostly back on my feet, I have decided to discuss some of these issues and to talk about some of the things that have helped me recover.

### Writing

Writing is also becoming a renewed focus for me recently. I've got a series of novels that I have been writing on-and-off for some time now, and I'm making a renewed effort to sit down and actually get the damn things finished. I thought I'd share some of my experiences, as well as some of the tips and advice that I've picked up along the way.

### Final Thoughts

This post is mainly for me to be honest; a statement of intent going forward. I'm hoping to get back into the habit of posting regularly over the next few months and hopefully some of those posts will be a bit more interesting!
