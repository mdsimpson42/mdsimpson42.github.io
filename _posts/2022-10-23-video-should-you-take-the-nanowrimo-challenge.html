With November fast approaching, you might be considering taking part in the National Novel Writing Month (NaNoWriMo) Challenge. But what is it like to take part? In this video (my first 'proper' video and my first writing video on my YouTube channel), I talk about my experience of taking part in NaNoWriMo over the last three years.

I plan to post more video essays on my experiences, what I learned from NaNoWriMo, plus a video review of Scrivener, the software that I am using to write my novels, so be sure to subscribe and follow me if you want to see more.
