You didn't really think they'd cast them to play a bit part, did you?

The Legend of Vox Machina

The most recent example of this occurred in Season 4 of the Critical Role TV series The Legend of Vox Machina.

Spoilers for Vox Machina Season 4

The series focuses on a new villain called The Whispered One. Early on in the series, they introduce a brand new character who just happens to be played by Andy Serkis. A big name actor playing a brand new character, just after they introduced the new villain? I guess we know who the whispered one is!
