Posts on [mental health](/categories/mental-health.html), focussing on a range of topics where I think it is
important that we continue the conversation. Some of these topics are quite personal, and may be triggering,
but I believe that it is important talk about them, as it can help people feel seen, realise they are not alone,
and encourage people to ask for help when they need it.

This became the basis for a similar series of blog posts that I created as part of
[the ConveRSE project](https://mdsimpson42.github.io/converse).
