A series of blog posts I wrote about my experience of becoming a father. My original goal was to write about my experience,
share interesting things that I'd learned along the way, and to document the journey of becoming a parent for myself and
other first-time dads. My son was then born in April 2020, and so it became a lockdown parenting blog, and I wrote about
a range of topics that I didn't expect to write about, including [mental health](/categories/mental-health.html).
